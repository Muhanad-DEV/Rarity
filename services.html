Our Services

What We Do

Our comprehensive approach to sustainable fashion and ethical labor

Upcycling & Sustainability

At Rarity, we transform fast-fashion waste into modern, durable clothes through our innovative upcycling process:

  • Collection of discarded garments from local factories
  • Sorting and cleaning of materials
  • Creative redesign and reconstruction
  • Skilled craftsmanship by local artisans
  • Eco-friendly dyeing and finishing processes

Each piece we create diverts textiles from landfills while creating beautiful, one-of-a-kind garments that tell a story.

Fair Labor Practices

We're committed to ethical employment that respects the dignity and rights of every worker:

  • Living wages that exceed local standards
  • Safe, clean working environments
  • Reasonable working hours and paid leave
  • Skills training and career development
  • Healthcare benefits for workers and families

Our workers are primarily from marginalized backgrounds, including women who previously worked in exploitative conditions in the fast-fashion industry.

Community Outreach

We extend our impact beyond employment through targeted community initiatives:

  • Donation of upcycled clothing to those in need
  • Focus on underserved areas: Dhaka slums (Korail, Hazaribagh), Kurigram, and Gaibandha
  • Educational workshops on sustainable fashion
  • Community engagement and empowerment programs
  • Environmental cleanup initiatives

Through these efforts, we aim to create lasting positive change in communities affected by poverty and environmental degradation.
