Our locations and service areas in Bangladesh

Our Service Areas

Dhaka Slums

Our primary production facility is located near Korail, one of Dhaka's largest slums, where we employ local residents. We also serve the Hazaribagh area.

Kurigram

Our northern Bangladesh operations focus on this region, which faces high poverty rates and limited employment opportunities.

Gaibandha

We've recently expanded to Gaibandha, providing training and employment to local garment workers while distributing upcycled clothing to those in need.

Planning a Visit?

If you're interested in visiting our facilities or learning more about our work firsthand, please contact us to arrange a tour. We welcome visitors who want to see the impact of our work and meet the talented artisans behind our upcycled creations.
